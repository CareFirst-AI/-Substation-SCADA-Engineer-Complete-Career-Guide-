What is a Substation SCADA Engineer?

A Substation SCADA Engineer leads the design, development, and delivery of Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) systems for high-voltage electrical substations. This role combines electrical engineering expertise with advanced control systems knowledge to ensure reliable power transmission and distribution infrastructure.
